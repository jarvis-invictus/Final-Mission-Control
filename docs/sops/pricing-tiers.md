- ✅ SEO fundamentals (title tags, meta descriptions, schema)
- ✅ Contact form with email notifications

- ✅ Google Maps integration
- ✅ SSL certificate

- ✅ Basic Google Analytics setup

### What's NOT Included:
- ❌ CRM / lead management
- ❌ Automated follow-ups
- ❌ Review management
- ❌ Social media setup
- ❌ Ongoing updates
- ❌ Ad management

- Solo practitioner or new business
- "I just need a website"
- Limited marketing budget
- Handling 0-20 clients/month
- Currently has no website or very outdated one

### Upsell Path:
After 30-60 days, follow up with: "Your website generated [X] form submissions this month. Imagine if those were automatically followed up with a text within 60 seconds — that's our Growth package."

## Niche-Specific Pricing Notes

### Dental
- **Sweet spot:** Growth tier (₹40,000 setup + ₹10,000/mo)
- **Key sell:** "New patient pipeline + no-show reduction + review growth"
- **Average dental patient LTV:** ₹15,000-50,000 → system pays for itself with 1 new patient/month

### CA/Tax
- **Sweet spot:** Growth tier (₹35,000 setup + ₹8,000/mo)
- **Key sell:** "Client onboarding automation + document collection + deadline reminders"
- **Average CA client LTV:** ₹10,000-30,000/year → system pays for itself with 1 new client/month
- **Seasonal upsell:** Offer ITR season boost package (Jan-Mar) at ₹15,000 one-time

### Education/Coaching
- **Sweet spot:** Starter or Growth (depends on size)
- **Key sell:** "Enrollment pipeline + batch management + parent communication"
- **Average student LTV:** ₹20,000-2,00,000 → even Starter pays for itself quickly
- **Note:** Many coaching centers are price-sensitive — lead with Starter, upsell later

### Lawyers
- **Sweet spot:** Growth tier (₹45,000 setup + ₹12,000/mo)
- **Key sell:** "Professional intake forms + confidential lead management + authority content"
- **Average legal client value:** ₹25,000-5,00,000 → highest ROI niche
- **Note:** Lawyers value professionalism — premium pricing works better here

## Payment Terms

### Starter Tier
- **Option A:** 100% upfront before build starts
- **Option B:** 50% upfront + 50% before go-live
- No monthly commitment

### Growth Tier
- 50% setup fee upfront
- 50% setup fee on go-live
- Monthly recurring starts Day 1 of go-live
- 3-month minimum commitment
- Cancel anytime after 3 months with 30-day notice

### Scale Tier
- 40% setup fee upfront
- 30% at first review milestone
- 30% on go-live
- Monthly recurring starts Day 1 of go-live
- 6-month minimum commitment
- Cancel anytime after 6 months with 30-day notice

### Payment Methods:
- Razorpay payment links (UPI, credit/debit card, net banking)
- Direct bank transfer (NEFT/IMPS)
- No cash payments
- No post-dated cheques
