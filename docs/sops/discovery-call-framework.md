# Discovery Call Framework — Invictus AI
## Universal 15-30 Minute Framework (All Niches)

---

## Pre-Call Research Checklist (5 min before call)
- [ ] Google their business name — check website, GMB listing, reviews
- [ ] Check Google Maps rating + review count
- [ ] Screenshot their current website (or note "no website")
- [ ] Check social media: Instagram, Facebook page, LinkedIn
- [ ] Note competitor websites in same area (2-3)
- [ ] Check if they're running Google Ads (search their service + city)
- [ ] Prepare 2-3 personalized observations ("I noticed your Google rating is 4.2 with 45 reviews…")

---

## Phase 1: Opening (2-3 minutes)

### Script:
> "Hi [Name], thanks for taking the time today! I'm [Agent] from Invictus AI. Before we jump in, I want to make sure this call is valuable for you — I'll ask some questions about your business, share what we've seen work for [niche] businesses like yours, and if it makes sense, we'll talk about next steps. Sound good?"

### Key Goals:
- Build rapport (comment on something specific about their business)
- Set agenda and time expectation
- Get verbal buy-in to proceed

---

## Phase 2: Current State Assessment (5-8 minutes)

### Core Questions (ALL niches):

**Online Presence:**
1. "Do you currently have a website? When was it last updated?"
2. "How do most of your new customers/clients find you right now?"
3. "Are you listed on Google Maps? Do you actively manage your Google Business Profile?"
4. "How many Google reviews do you have, and what's your rating?"

**Lead Generation:**
5. "On average, how many new enquiries do you get per week/month?"
6. "What percentage of those convert to paying customers/clients?"
7. "Are you running any ads — Google, Facebook, Instagram?"

**Operations:**
8. "How do you currently manage appointments/bookings?"
9. "Do you send follow-up messages to customers after their visit?"
10. "How do you handle missed calls or after-hours enquiries?"

### Listen For:
- **Pain signals:** "We lose leads", "Our website is outdated", "We don't follow up"
- **Opportunity signals:** "We're growing", "We want more visibility", "Competitors are ahead"
- **Budget signals:** "We tried [expensive thing]", references to marketing spend

---

## Phase 3: Needs Discovery (5-8 minutes)

### Core Questions:

**Goals:**
1. "If we could solve ONE thing for your business in the next 90 days, what would it be?"
2. "Where do you want your business to be in 12 months?"
3. "What's stopping you from getting there right now?"

**Decision Process:**
4. "Besides yourself, is there anyone else involved in making this decision?"
5. "Have you worked with a marketing agency or web designer before? How was that experience?"
6. "What's your timeline — are you looking to get started soon, or more exploring options?"

**Budget Signals (indirect):**
7. "What are you currently spending on marketing/advertising per month?"
8. "If we could guarantee [specific result], what would that be worth to your business?"

### Scoring During Call:
| Factor | Score (1-5) | Notes |
|--------|-------------|-------|
| Urgency | _ | How soon do they need this? |
| Budget Fit | _ | Can they afford our services? |
| Decision Authority | _ | Can they say yes today? |
| Current Pain | _ | How much is the problem costing them? |
| Technical Readiness | _ | Do they have domain, content, etc.? |

**Lead Score = Total / 25 × 100%**
- 80%+ → HOT — push for same-week close
- 60-79% → WARM — nurture with follow-up
- Below 60% → COLD — add to drip sequence

---

## Phase 4: Solution Presentation (5 minutes)

### Framework: Problem → Solution → Proof → Offer

> "Based on what you've told me, here's what I see: [restate their top 2-3 pain points]. 
> 
> What we do at Invictus AI is build complete digital systems for [niche] businesses — not just a website, but everything that turns online visitors into paying customers.
> 
> Here's specifically what that looks like for you:
> 1. **Professional AI-powered website** that's designed specifically for [niche] — fast, mobile-optimized, built to convert visitors into leads
> 2. **Automated follow-ups** — when someone fills out your form or misses a call, they automatically get a text/email within seconds
> 3. **Review management** — automated review requests after every [appointment/service/consultation]
> 4. **CRM + pipeline** — see every lead, where they are, what's next — all in one dashboard
> 
> We've built this for [X] businesses in [city/niche]. The typical result is [specific metric]."

### Niche-Specific Value Props:

**Dental:**
- "Your website becomes a 24/7 appointment booking machine"
- "Patients get automatic reminders — reduces no-shows by 30-40%"
- "Automated review requests after every visit — dentists average 4.5+ stars within 3 months"

**CA/Tax:**
- "Clients can book consultations directly — no phone tag"
- "Document collection forms built in — clients upload everything before the meeting"
- "Seasonal campaign automations — ITR reminders, GST deadlines, tax-saving tips"

**Education/Coaching:**
- "Lead capture for free workshops/webinars → automatic nurture sequence"
- "Student enrollment forms + automated onboarding"
- "Testimonial collection + showcase system"

**Lawyers:**
- "Confidential intake forms — GDPR-compliant, professional"
- "Case type routing — family law, criminal, corporate → right lawyer"
- "Authority content system — blog/articles that rank on Google for [city] + [practice area]"

---

## Phase 5: Objection Handling

### "It's too expensive"
> "I understand — let me ask: how much is it costing you NOT to have this? If you're losing even 5 leads a month at ₹[average ticket], that's ₹[X] per month in lost revenue. Our system pays for itself within [timeframe]."

### "I need to think about it"
> "Absolutely, this is an important decision. What specifically would you want to think over? [Address specific concern]. Would it help if I sent you a case study of a [niche] business we've worked with?"

### "I already have a website"
> "That's great! Let me ask — when was it last updated? Is it generating leads consistently? Most businesses we work with HAD a website, but it wasn't working as a sales tool. Can I share my screen and show you what I noticed about your current site?"

### "Bad experience with agencies before"
> "I hear that a lot, honestly. That's exactly why we do things differently — you get a dedicated AI system, not a freelancer who disappears. Everything is built to run automatically. And we don't just hand you a website and walk away — we set up the entire system and train you on it."

### "We're too busy right now"
> "I get it — and that's actually the best time to set this up. The system runs itself once it's built. We handle everything — you just need 30 minutes for onboarding and one review session. When you ARE ready to grow, the machine is already running."

---

## Phase 6: Close / Next Steps (2-3 minutes)

### If HOT (score 80%+):
> "Based on everything we discussed, I think the [Growth/Scale] package is the perfect fit. Here's what I recommend — let's get you onboarded this week. I'll send over the agreement and onboarding form today. Once you complete that, we start building within 48 hours. Does [day] work to kick things off?"

### If WARM (60-79%):
> "I'll put together a custom proposal based on what we discussed and send it over by [tomorrow]. I'll include the specific deliverables, timeline, and pricing. Can we schedule a quick 10-minute follow-up call for [2-3 days from now] to go over it?"

### If COLD (below 60%):
> "I appreciate your time today. Let me send you some resources — a case study from a [niche] business similar to yours, and our capabilities overview. When you're ready to move forward, you'll have everything you need. I'll follow up in [2 weeks] to see where things stand."

---

## Post-Call Checklist
- [ ] Update CRM: move to correct pipeline stage
- [ ] Log call notes with lead score
- [ ] Send follow-up email within 1 hour:
  - Thank them for their time
  - Recap key points discussed
  - Include proposal/case study/next steps
  - Clear CTA (book onboarding / review proposal / schedule follow-up)
- [ ] Set follow-up task in CRM with specific date
- [ ] If WON → trigger onboarding sequence
- [ ] If proposal sent → set Day 2 + Day 5 follow-up reminders

## Niche-Specific Add-On Questions

### Dental
- "How many chairs do you have? What's your current patient volume per day?"
- "Do you offer cosmetic/aesthetic treatments, or mainly general dentistry?"
- "What's your average treatment value?"
- "Are you accepting new patients, or is the goal to fill specific time slots?"

### CA/Tax
- "How many active clients do you manage currently?"
- "What's your split between GST/ITR/audit/advisory work?"
- "Do you serve primarily individuals, businesses, or both?"
- "Is there a seasonal pattern — ITR rush, GST quarters?"
- "Do your clients interact with you digitally at all, or everything is in-person/phone?"

### Education/Coaching
- "What's your average batch size? How many batches per month?"
- "Do you offer online, offline, or hybrid classes?"
- "What's your enrollment process like currently?"
- "Where do most students hear about you?"
- "What's the lifetime value of a student (full course fee)?"

### Lawyers
- "What practice areas do you focus on?"
- "How do potential clients typically reach you — referrals, Google, word of mouth?"
- "Do you offer free initial consultations?"
- "What's your average case value?"
- "How important is confidentiality in your intake process?"
